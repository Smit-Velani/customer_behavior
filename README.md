📊 Customer Behavior Analysis – End-to-End Data Analytics Project

This project analyzes retail customer shopping behavior to uncover insights that help businesses improve sales performance, customer engagement, and long-term loyalty. The project follows a complete data analytics workflow, including data preparation, SQL analysis, and dashboard visualization.

🚀 Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand how customers shop and what factors influence purchasing behavior.

Management observed changes in:

      Customer demographics
      Product category performance
      Online vs. offline shopping behavior

They want to identify the impact of factors such as:

      Discounts
      Product reviews
      Purchase history
      Shipping preferences
      Customer demographics
      Core Business Question

How can the company use consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

🛠 Tools Used

Python → Data preparation and modeling
SQL → Business analysis and querying
Power BI → Interactive dashboard visualization

⚙️ Project Workflow :

1️⃣ Data Preparation & Modeling (Python)

Import and explore the dataset
Clean and transform raw data
Prepare structured data for analysis
Create variables used for analytics (e.g., age groups)

2️⃣ Data Analysis (SQL)

SQL queries were used to answer key business questions:

Total revenue by gender
Customers who used discounts but spent above average
Top 5 products with highest review ratings
Comparison of standard vs express shipping purchases
Spending comparison between subscribers and non-subscribers
Products with the highest discount usage rate
Customer segmentation into New, Returning, Loyal
Top 3 most purchased products in each category
Relationship between repeat buyers and subscriptions
Revenue contribution by age group

3️⃣ Dashboard Visualization (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard was created to visualize insights including:

Customer revenue distribution
Product performance
Customer segments
Purchase behavior trends
Age group revenue contribution
The dashboard allows stakeholders to quickly explore key metrics and patterns.

📊 Key Insights

Certain age groups contribute more revenue than others
Top-rated products tend to perform better in sales
Discounts influence purchasing behavior
Repeat buyers show stronger engagement with the business
Express shipping customers often show higher purchasing activity

💡Business Recommendations 

Boost Subscriptions
Promote benefits that encourage customers to subscribe.

Customer Loyalty Programs
Reward repeat buyers to move them into the loyal segment.

Review Discount Strategy
Balance discounts with profitability.

Product Positioning
Promote top-rated and best-selling products in campaigns.

Targeted Marketing
Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue age groups and express-shipping users.
